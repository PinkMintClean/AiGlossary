The Human Physique: Somatotypes and Body Shapes

This section outlines a two-part classification system for the human physique: Somatotypes and Body Shapes. This framework provides a comprehensive lexicon for describing a subject's form.

Descriptor Category Key Characteristics Example Use in Prompt
